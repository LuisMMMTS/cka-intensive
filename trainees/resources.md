## Exam-day tips

- Tested ID: passport or government ID with Latin characters
- Quiet, well-lit room; clean desk; one monitor; one webcam
- The PSI proctor will scan your room — clear it 15 min before start
- Browser: only Chrome works for the proctoring software
- Two failed identity checks = exam terminated, no refund — be ready early

## Post-CKA

- **CKS** (Certified Kubernetes Security Specialist) — natural follow-up if you do platform work
- **KCSA** — if you want a security cert without CKA prerequisite
- **CKAD** — if you want the dev-side complement
